Amazing Rock Paper Scissors Game!

This is a traditional rock, paper, scissors game, only on the computer. The rules are simple: Each round you can click on the rock, the paper, or the scissors to play it. If you would rather, you can press the 'r', 'p', or 's' keys on your keyboard to select your play. The computer will choose randomly.

Who wins? Well, rock will smash scissors, paper will cover rock, and scissors will cut paper; so each object has a chance of winning or losing. And if you and the computer both play the same object, it is a tie - nobody wins or loses.
